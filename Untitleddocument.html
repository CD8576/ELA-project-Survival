About the Main Character

of House Arrest - by K.A. Holt

I don’t think they ever said there name much in the book

In the book the main character is under house arrest

and they have to write in a diary to show there a good person and can stay out of juvie

How they got into that mess needs a bit of explanation

They have a sibling lexi that has a condition where they cant breath, and needed to have a hole cut in their throat to be able to breathe, and they need expensive meds.

His mother is not in the best financial situation mainly because of the meds.

His father left for the milk and never came back after he could not take it.

So one day when He was at the store a man got in an accident and the main character saw they dropped their wallet and seeing the opportunity they stole it in desperation and used the money to buy the meds for lexie.

And they almost got away with it but they got caught and sent to juvie for a bit.

Then they were let free, but on House Arrest and made to write in a diary for a year to show they are a good person.
